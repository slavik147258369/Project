WHO WE ARE?

Yellow Gears is software development agency from Ukraine.
We provide mobile applications development services on
Upwork from March 2016. Our clients are happy start-ups
of small, mid and large sizes all over the world.

OUR EXPERIANCE

We delivered about 50 projects to our clients:
- Developed from scratch more than 30 projects
- Completed and successfully launched more than
10 complex legacy projects which were not completed
by others and we are proud of this

PROJECT TYPES WE HAVE WORKED ON:

- Augmented Reality native mobile apps
- Multivendor marketplaces
- Car sharing
- Kiosk apps
- Social networking apps
- Bluetooth connection apps
- Geofencing and geolocation apps
- Hardware monitoring and management
apps for factories and mining industry

WE ARE FLEXIBLE AND AGILE BECAUSEL

1) We use theory of constraints and 80/20 rule in project management to set priorities correctly, this saves time and effort maximally.
2) We are opened for new information and use new technologies to improve productivity
3) We provide builds, work reports and repository commits as often as you want
4) Project teams can be augmented by new reliable developers to increase development speed
5) Requirements changes are applied on the run within a sprint to avoid redundant wastes
6) Our working hours and online presence depends on you
7) We are able to use various tools for task management and CI/CD
